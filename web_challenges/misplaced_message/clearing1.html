Clearings

Clearings act as natural solar patches where the sudden influx of light triggers a high-speed growth race among dormant seeds waiting on the forest floor. These open spaces are often maintained by large herbivores like bison or elephants whose constant grazing and trampling prevent the surrounding woodland from reclaiming the gap.
