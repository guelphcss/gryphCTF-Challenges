The Royal Blacksmith is famous for his legendary blades, created by folding two separate materials together in a very specific order, known exclusively by blacksmiths of such caliber. A rival forge stole pages from his ledger, which contains the blueprints for the King's new greatsword, but the letters have been hammered into an unreadable state.

It is rumoured that the blacksmith always tempers his best weapons with the same secret four letter word.
